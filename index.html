How AutoSOS Works

1. Detecting the Crash

AutoSOS detects impact using advanced motion and pressure sensors. It can identify frontal, side, rear, and rollover crashes.

2. Verifying the Crash

Built-in front and rear cameras analyze the accident. If confirmed, AutoSOS moves to the next step.

3. Sending Emergency Alerts

AutoSOS immediately alerts 911 and sends your location to family members.

4. No Signal? No Problem!

If no network is available, AutoSOS will try to use nearby phones to send the alert. If that fails, it will use iPhone 14 Pro+ satellite communication.

5. Attracting Nearby Help

If emergency services are delayed, AutoSOS activates a loud alarm and flashing lights to signal for nearby assistance.
